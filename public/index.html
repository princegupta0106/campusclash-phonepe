❓ FAQ & Support

Help Center

Find answers to common questions about Campus Clash.

How do I register for a tournament? +

First, create an account or log in. Then browse the available tournaments on the home page, click on one you'd like to join, and click the "Register Now" button. You'll be directed to the payment page to complete your registration.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We currently accept payments via PhonePe (UPI). More payment options including credit/debit cards and other UPI apps will be added soon.

Can I get a refund after registering? +

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you need to cancel your registration, please contact us at support@nextbytetech.com at least 48 hours before the tournament starts.

How do I know when a tournament starts? +

Tournament details including date, time, and venue are displayed on each tournament's page. You can also check the "My Tournaments" section after logging in to see all your registered tournaments.

Who organizes these tournaments? +

Campus Clash tournaments are organized by NextByte Technologies in collaboration with college clubs and gaming communities. If you'd like to organize a tournament, reach out to us!

Is there a team size requirement? +

Team size requirements vary by game. For example, Valorant requires a team of 5, BGMI requires a squad of 4, while FIFA and Chess are individual competitions. Check each tournament's details for specific requirements.
